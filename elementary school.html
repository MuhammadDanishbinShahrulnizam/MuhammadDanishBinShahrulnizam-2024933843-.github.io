Elementary School

I studied at Sekolah Kebangsaan Khir Johari (SK Khir Johari) during my elementary school years. This was where I learned the basic foundations of education such as reading, writing, and counting, while also developing good values like discipline, responsibility, and respect.

The teachers were supportive and caring, and the school environment helped me grow both academically and personally, making my time at SK Khir Johari a meaningful part of my educational journey.

These early years shap ed my character and prepared me for secondary school and beyond. The memories from elementary school will always remain a meaningful part of my life.
