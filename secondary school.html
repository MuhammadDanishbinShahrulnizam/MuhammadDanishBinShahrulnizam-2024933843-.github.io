Secondary School

I studied at Sekolah Menengah Integrasi Teras Islam At-Taqwa (SMITI At-Taqwa) during my secondary school years. The school provided an integrated education that combined academic subjects with Islamic studies, helping me build a strong foundation in knowledge and faith.

The learning environment encouraged discipline, responsibility, and respect, which shaped my character and mindset as a student.

Throughout my time at SMITI At-Taqwa, I was guided by dedicated teachers who emphasized both personal growth and academic achievement. The school also helped me develop good values, self-confidence, and a sense of purpose, preparing me to face future challenges with balance between worldly knowledge and spiritual understanding.
